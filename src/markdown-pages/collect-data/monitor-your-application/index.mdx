<Intro>

You're the developer of FoodMe, an Angular app that lets users order food from their favorite restaurants. Your app is very popular among the users. But right now, you have no visibility into how the app is performing. 
Surprised by the spike in popularity, you decided you should start monitoring your application.

In this lab, you instrument your application with New Relic agents and monitor its metrics, logs, events, and transactions.


</Intro>

<FlexWrapper>

<Objectives>

## Objectives

- Spin up an angular demo application
- Install New Relic APM, and browser agents 
- Monitor your application with New Relic

</Objectives>

<Requirements>

## Requirements

- A free [New Relic account](https://newrelic.com/signup?utm_source=developer-site)
- [Node](https://nodejs.org/en/)

</Requirements>
